About our project

Inspired by initiatives in Israel, Paris, and Melbourne, we are crafting a public art piece with yellow ribbons in collaboration with artist Sarah Divi. Each Stitch Them Home installation will feature yellow yarn ribbons, lovingly stitched by hand and prominently displayed, to bring a call for the safe return of those taken hostage.
