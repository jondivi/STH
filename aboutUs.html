About

With the help of local crafters,
Sarah Divi and Beautifully Jewish created this community project.

Sarah Divi (@sarahdiviart) creates unique fiber artwork, transforming walls, fences, and city blocks into captivating, immersive experiences.

Beautifully Jewish is a project of Tablet Magazine that celebrates the Jewish objects we love and the people who make them. Created by Stephanie Butnick and Tanya Singer, Beautifully Jewish is a podcast that grew into a community that hosts virtual craft-alongs and meaningful conversations and organizes projects supporting Israel.
